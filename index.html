What is it?

Sheep Herder is a free casual game developed by Team Sheepy. In this game you have to lead as much sheep as possible, as fast as possible to their pen. The more sheep you get in there, the higher your score.


Who are we?

We are three student at the KU Leuven, developping this game as a project for the course "Fundamentals of CHI". You can follow our adventures on our blog. Let us know if you have remarks/ideas for improvements/...
